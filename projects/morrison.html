THE CHALLENGE

Repositioning for Growth

Morrison & Associates had built a strong reputation serving established corporations, but the partners saw an opportunity to pivot toward the tech startup ecosystem. Their existing brand felt corporate and dated—misaligned with the founders they wanted to attract.

They needed a complete transformation: new positioning that spoke to startup founders, a modern visual identity that stood out from traditional law firm aesthetics, and a digital presence that could compete with the best in tech.

Brand Strategy Workshop
Competitive Analysis
THE SOLUTION

Strategic Repositioning

We developed a new brand platform centered on the concept of "Legal Partners for Builders"—positioning Morrison as the law firm that truly understands the startup journey because they're entrepreneurs themselves.

The visual identity moved away from traditional law firm clichés toward a clean, modern aesthetic that feels at home in the tech ecosystem while maintaining credibility and trust.
