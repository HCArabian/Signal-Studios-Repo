[ 005 ] MEET THE FOUNDER

Hi, I'm Chris.

I started Signal Studios because I was tired of seeing talented businesses struggle with the same problem: they're great at what they do, but their brand doesn't show it.

Before this, I spent years in web development and saw how often companies got stuck between expensive agencies that didn't understand their business and DIY solutions that looked... well, DIY. There had to be a better way.

So I built Signal Studios to be what I wish existed—a creative partner that actually listens, genuinely cares about results, and makes the whole process feel collaborative instead of transactional.

I work with a small network of designers and strategists I trust completely. We keep things intentionally small so every client gets real attention, not just a seat on an assembly line.
